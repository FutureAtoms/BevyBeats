BevyBeats is a premium Music AI app that plays and generates AI music with the following features:

- Premium AI music generation and playback
- Browsing Home, Charts, Podcast, Moods & Genre with high speed
- Search and discover new music easily
- Analyze your playing data, create custom playlists
- Spotify Canvas support
- Play 1080p video with subtitle support
- AI-generated music suggestions
- Notification from followed artists
- Caching and offline playback
- Synced lyrics from multiple sources with translation
- Personalize music data and support multiple accounts
- Sleep Timer
- Android Auto with online content
- And many more!

Developed by Abhilash Chadhar (FutureAtoms)
